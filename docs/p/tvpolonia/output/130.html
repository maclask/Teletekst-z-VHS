P130130 02/04 TVP pt.22.11 21 47/41 NEWS FROM POLAND * NEWS FROM POLAND President Aleksander Kwasniewski has appointed Lt. Gen. Zbigniew Zalewski to the newly created post oV the Army Commander. Thus all Forces (the Army, the Navy, the Air Force) have their commanders now, that are directly subordinated to the civilian defence minister. According to the old (Soviet style) pattern of organization of The Highest Command, the Army was divided between 4 big territorial military districts with no nationwide commander. Command- ers of districts were subordinated to the military Chief of the General Staff (Commander-in-Chief during a war). The reform is to make Polish Army more compatible with its future NATO counterparts, and to strenghten the civilian, democratic control over the armed forces. (PAP,pk)
